Mob Translate

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 29 June 2026

Mob Translate is an open-source app for learning, translating and recording Australian First Nations languages. This policy explains what we collect and how we use it. We keep it simple because we collect as little as possible.

What we collect

  • Account details — your name and email address, only if you create an account. These are used to sign you in and to attribute the recordings you contribute.
  • Voice recordings & sentences — if you choose to record words or sentences, the audio and the text you enter are sent to Mob Translate so they can become part of the community dictionary.
  • Basic technical data — standard request information (such as device type) needed to deliver the service. We do not use advertising trackers.

What you type while translating or searching is sent to our server only to return a result. The bundled keyboard does its word suggestions on your device — what you type on the keyboard is not uploaded.

How we use it

  • To provide translation, dictionary, pronunciation and recording features.
  • To build community language dictionaries and preserve languages.
  • To let you and language workers review the recordings you contribute.

What we don't do

  • We do not sell your data.
  • We do not share your personal information with advertisers.
  • We do not use your data for advertising profiles.

Community language & data

Language belongs to its community. Recordings and language content are treated as community knowledge, handled with respect to the wishes of the relevant language community and custodians. If you contribute a recording you can ask for it to be removed.

Storage & security

Data is sent over encrypted connections (HTTPS) and stored on our servers. Recordings are retained so they remain available in the dictionary unless you ask us to delete them.

Your choices & deletion

You can ask us to delete your account, your recordings, or any data we hold about you. Email us and we will action it.

Children

The app is for general audiences and is suitable for learners of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children beyond what is described above.
