Mob Translate — Google Play kit

Everything to publish the app on the Play Store. Tap Copy on any field and paste it into the matching box in Play Console. Download the images and upload them.

You need a Google Play Developer account first (one-time $25, with ID verification). Then create the app in play.google.com/console and paste these in. The app file (.aab) is provided separately.

ASteps in Play Console

  1. Sign in to Play Console → Create app. App name: Mob Translate. Default language: English (Australia). App or game: App. Free or paid: Free. Accept the declarations.
  2. Store listing (Grow → Store presence → Main store listing): paste the App name, Short description, Full description below. Upload the App icon, Feature graphic, and Phone screenshots from the Images section.
  3. Store settings: App category = Education; add tags; enter the contact email and website; set the Privacy policy URL (below).
  4. Policy → App content: complete Privacy policy, Data safety (use the answers below), Content rating questionnaire (answers below), Target audience, Ads (No ads), Government apps (No).
  5. Production → Create release (or test on Internal testing first): upload the .aab, paste the Release notes, review and roll out.
