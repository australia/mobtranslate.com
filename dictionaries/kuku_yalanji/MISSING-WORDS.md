Twelve reader agents harvested **1,977 glossed lexeme attestations** from Patz's reference grammar (every interlinear example, paradigm table, and the appendix narrative texts). A generous, morphology-aware diff against the dictionary left **104 candidate roots**; de-duplication (→99 lemmas) and an adversarial verification pass **accepted 65** and **rejected 34** (inflected forms, regular derivations, spelling variants). Two accepted pairs were then merged as spelling variants, leaving **63 new lexemes** added to `dictionary.yaml` (marked `source: grammar`).
